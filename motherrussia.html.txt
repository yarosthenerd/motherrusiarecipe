    <h1>Crepés "Mother Russia" Style</h1>
    <p>Delicious thin crepes for breakfast or desert</p>
    <section>
      <h2>Ingredients</h2>
      <ul>
        <li>250g of flour</li>
        <li>500ml of water</li>
        <li>1 teaspoon of salt</li>
        <li>2 tablespoons of sugar</li>
        <li>2 eggs</li>
        <li>3 tablespoons of plant-based oil <em>(for bubbles)</em></li>
        <li>100g of unsalted butter <em>(not required, a topping variant)</em></li>
      </ul>
      <section>
        <img src="https://cdn.freecodecamp.org/curriculum/labs/recipe.jpg" alt="Eggs and flour.">
      </section>
    </section>
    <section>
      <h2>Instructions</h2>
      <ol>
        <li>Pour the water into a bowl and slowly add flour, mixing <strong>constantly</strong>.</li>
        <li>Add the eggs, sugar and salt.</li>
        <li>Add the oil <em>(I prefer olive)</em> to the mixture.</li>
        <li>Preheat the pan.</li>
        <li>Add a table spoon of oil to the pan and distribute it evenly.</li>
        <li>Pour some mixture into the pan <em>(just enough to cover the bottom)</em>.</li>
        <li>Allow the crepe to bake for 30-60 seconds.</li>
        <li>Use a metal/wooden spatula to flip the crepe. <strong>Be careful, don't tear it!</strong></li>
        <li>Bake the crepe for another 30-60 seconds.</li>
        <li>Use the spatula to place the crepe on a plate.</li>
        <li>Add some butter on the top and sprinkle some sugar. <em>It will melt over time and turn into a syrup!</em></li>
        <li>Repeat the steps 6-11 until you have no mixture left.</li>
      </ol>
